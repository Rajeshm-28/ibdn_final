National Conference on Empowerment of PwDs

Transforming Ideas into Action – Stepping into the Next Decade

5 March 2020, Hotel Leela Palace, New Delhi

About the Event

CII is organizing the National Conference on Empowerment of PwDs, to discuss and deliberate on possible action to enable inclusion at workplace in the coming decade - ‘Transforming ideas into Action- Stepping into the next decade’.

Creating inclusive and resilient environment is a task that requires collaborated and coherent efforts and continued exchange of communication and dialogues by various stakeholders. The conference will offer the platform to voice opinions, views and thoughts of those who are engaged and impacted.
