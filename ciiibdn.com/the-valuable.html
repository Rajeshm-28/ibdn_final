India Inc | The Valuable 500

Passionate Leaders. One Vision. One Commitment.
To Drive Value Through Disability Inclusion.

29 November 2019 @1000hrs Leela Palace, New Delhi

About Event

Brookfield Properties and Confederation of Indian Industry (CII)- India Business and Disability Network (IBDN) in association with EnAble India invites you to an exclusive immersive event “India Inc | The Valuable 500” on Friday, 29 November 2019 at New Delhi.

The Valuable 500 is a global campaign that was launched at the World Economic Forum this year. The Valuable 500, founded by Caroline Casey, aims to bring together 500 CEOs to commit to get disability inclusion included in the boardroom agenda around the world.

The event will bring together companies and key stakeholders who are championing and committed to disability inclusion and are positively impacting the eco-system.
